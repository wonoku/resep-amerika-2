Ingredients of Bread Machine Chalah

  1. Prepare 1 cup of water.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of Sugar.
  3. Prepare 5 Tbs of Honey.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of Vegetable Oilg.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Salt.
  6. It's 2 of Eggs.
  7. You need 4 cups of Bread Flour.
  8. Prepare 3 tsp of Yeast.
  9. It's 1 of Egg, beaten.

Bread Machine Chalah instructions

  1. Add ingredients(except beaten egg) in bread machine.x.
  2. Set machine to DOUGH CYCLE and hit START..
  3. Watch dough knead as you might have to add a bit of water or flour..
  4. Towards the end if the DOUGH CYCLE rise you might have to punch down dough as it rises a lot..
  5. At the end of the DOUGH CYCLE take out dough and split it in two..
  6. Take each half and cut it in 3 pieces..
  7. Braid dough pieces..
  8. Place braids on cookie pan lined with parchment paper..
  9. Cover with a damp cloth..
  10. Set oven to 350 and let braids rise for 1 hour..
  11. Beat egg in small bowl..
  12. Uncover braids and brush egg on Chalahs..
  13. Place in oven for 40 minutes..
  14. Take sharp knife and poke center. You want the knife to come up dry. If not dry put back in oven for 5 minutes..
  15. Let Chalahs cool..
  16. Shabbat shalom!!.
