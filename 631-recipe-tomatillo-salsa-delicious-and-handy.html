Ingredients of Tomatillo Salsa

  1. It's 2-3 handfuls of Tomatillos.
  2. It's 2 of fresh jalapeños.
  3. You need 2 cloves of garlic.
  4. You need 1/4 of chopped onion.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of cilantro.
  6. You need 4 teaspoons of seasoned salt.

Tomatillo Salsa step by step

  1. Roast tomatillos, jalapeños and garlic in foil..
  2. Put in blender and add about 4 tbs of seasoned salt. Blend..
  3. Chop onions and cilantro and mix in.
  4. Enjoy!.
