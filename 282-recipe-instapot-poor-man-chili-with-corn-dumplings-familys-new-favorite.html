Instapot Poor Man Chili with Corn Dumplings step by step

  1. Put your instapot on meat setting and fry up the meat, onions garlic, 1/2 of chili seasoning til meat is cooked.
  2. Add beans, soup, rest of seasoning. Stir.
  3. Mix up all dumplings ingredients well.
  4. Make 7 round dumpling balls.
  5. Place on top of chili.
  6. Cook on bean setting.
