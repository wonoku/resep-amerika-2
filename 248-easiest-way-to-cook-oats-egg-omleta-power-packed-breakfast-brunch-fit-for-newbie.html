Oats Egg Omlet(A Power Packed breakfast / brunch) step by step

  1. Coarse grind oats..
  2. In a big bowl mix all chopped vegetables..
  3. Add Salt, pepper and Oragano to it. Add cold Milk. Mix well..
  4. Now beat egg and add to the above mixture. Adjust salt..
  5. Heat a nonstick griddle, spread butter / olive oil. Spread Oats and Egg mixture..
  6. Cook covered for 2 minutes..
  7. Check if one side is done. And then flip over..
  8. Your Power Packed Omlet is ready to serve..
  9. Give your kid with Tomato ketchup and a glass of milk/ juice..
