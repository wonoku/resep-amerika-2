Ingredients of Potato salad

  1. Prepare 5 pounds of potatoes.
  2. You need 2-3 of celery.
  3. Prepare 2-3 of green onions.
  4. Prepare 6 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 1 can of olives.
  6. Prepare of Mayo.
  7. Prepare of Mustard.
  8. It's of Garlic powder.
  9. It's 4-5 of Dill pickles.
  10. It's of Salt and pepper.
  11. You need of Paprika.

Potato salad step by step

  1. Peel and cut them into bite size and wash them. And put them in the water and cook them till fork tender.
  2. Also start the eggs as well.
  3. While everything is cooking start the chopping of the celery pickles olives and green onions. And put them in the refrigerator...
  4. Drain the potatoes and put them in the refrigerator. Same with eggs.
  5. Let everything cool off for about an hour or 2. And then put the potatoes in a large bowl and add salt and pepper and mix..
  6. Add the veggie to the bowl as well. And add done big spoon fills of mayo and mix. Keep adding more may till you like it..
  7. Add the eggs to it and add the garlic and paprika to the mix... I like a lot of garlic so I add a lot but you can make it however you like it...
  8. Add some mustard to the mix and stir it nice and good..
  9. Serve it cold and it super easy and yummy... I make my macaroni salad like this as well but dont put in the mustard..
