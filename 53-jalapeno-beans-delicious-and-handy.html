Jalapeño Beans step by step

  1. Remove the seeds and ribbing of 2 jalapeño peppers and dice.
  2. Put into a pot with 2 cup water along with the pink Himalayan salt and seasoned salt..
  3. Add the parsley, paprika. Dice the 1/2 onion and add. Bring to a boil..
  4. After it has boiled for 10 minutes add butter and rice..
  5. Wash and remove seeds and ribbing from 3 jalapeño peppers and dice finely..
  6. Dice the 1-1/2 onions, carrots, and celery..
  7. Add these to the pork in a pan. Add the seasonings and brown the pork and Vegetables. Keep covered..
  8. Add 2 cup water. Cover the pot.
  9. When the carrots become softened add the beans and butter..
  10. Simmer covered for 20-25 minutes stirring occasionally. Then the rice and beans are done..
  11. Take the cornstarch and water and mix. Add to the beans and Pork. Stir till just thickened. Serve. I hope you enjoy!.
