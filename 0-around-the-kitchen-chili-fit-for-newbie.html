Ingredients of Around the Kitchen Chili

  1. It's 4 of whole boneless skinless chicken thighs, chopped.
  2. Prepare 4 of yellow potatoes chopped.
  3. It's 2 of whole portabella mushrooms, chopped.
  4. Prepare 1 of whole sweet onion diced.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of olive oil, extra virgin.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cumin.
  7. You need 1 tsp of chili powder.
  8. You need 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of onion powder.
  10. You need 3 of Roma tomatoes diced.
  11. You need 1 can of tomato sauce.
  12. It's 1 can of whole kernal corn.
  13. Prepare 2 cup of low sodium chicken broth.

Around the Kitchen Chili step by step

  1. Heat a large pot on Med-High heat and add Olive oil. Saute onion, mushrooms until slightly tender..
  2. Add chicken, spices and cook until slightly browned..
  3. Add tomato sauce, corn, tomatoes and potatoes..
  4. Cook until chicken is cooked through, stirring occasionally. Serve with your choice of side and topping..
