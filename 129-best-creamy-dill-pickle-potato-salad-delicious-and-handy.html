Ingredients of Creamy dill pickle potato salad

  1. You need 1 quart of red skin potatoes cut into bite size pieces.
  2. You need 2 tablespoons of chopped celery.
  3. Prepare 3 of spears Valley Brook Farms grape leaf dill pickle spears diced.
  4. You need 3 tablespoons of diced scallions (or red onion).
  5. Prepare 3 of heaping tablespoons mayonnaise.
  6. It's 2 teaspoons of Valley Brook Farms Flying pepper mustard.
  7. It's 1 teaspoon of juice from pickles.
  8. Prepare to taste of Fresh ground pepper.
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon of fresh dill finely chopped.

Creamy dill pickle potato salad instructions

  1. Bring large pot of well salted water to boil then add potatoes and cook till till tender (about 15 minutes). Drain and let cool while adding other ingredients to a large bowl. Then add potatoes and stir gently. Taste and adjust for seasoning..
  2. Cover salad and refrigerate up to an hour before serving.
