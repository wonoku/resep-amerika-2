Hearty Turkey Cassoulet instructions

  1. Put carrots in pot. Put enough water in to cover carrots. Bring to a boil. Boil just until crisp-tender; drain well..
  2. Cook sausage & turkey in a skillet until turkey is cooked. Drain. Reserving drippings..
  3. In large soup pot, put reserved drippings & oil. Heat over medium heat until hot. Add carrots, celery, & onion powder. Cook, stirring frequently, until tender..
  4. Stir in sausage, turkey, beans, undrained tomatoes, water, bouillon cubes, bay leaves, salt, & parsley..
  5. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes..
  6. Remove & discard the bay leaves..
  7. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top of soup. Serve..
