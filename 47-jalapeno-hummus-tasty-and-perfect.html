Ingredients of Jalapeno hummus

  1. It's 2 cans of garbanzo beans rinsed and drained.
  2. It's 2 of jalapeno peppers roughly chopped remove seeds for less heat.
  3. Prepare 1 bunch of cilantro, stems cut off.
  4. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic, peeled.
  5. It's of sesame oil, I started with 3/4 cup, add more to taste.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of water, if it is too thick add more.
  7. You need 1 of lime, juiced and zest.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of smoked paprika, more to taste.
  9. Prepare to taste of kosher salt.
