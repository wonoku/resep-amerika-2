Ingredients of Baking Bread with Bread Machine

  1. You need 1 teaspoon of olive oil.
  2. You need 300 ml of water.
  3. You need 650-700 gr of wholewheat flour.
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon of dry yeast.
  5. You need 2 teaspoon of sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon of salt.
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoon of flaxseed (optional).

Baking Bread with Bread Machine step by step

  1. Pour warm water and oil into pan.
  2. Add flour.
  3. Add sugar, salt, yeast.
  4. And flax seed.
  5. Set the machine for regular bread for 2 lbs (this bread machine cooks in 3 hours) number 1 set up for basic bread, hit the button and let the machine works for it.
  6. Bread is ready, take it out, upside down to a flat working surface.
  7. Here is the palette, you will take this off.
  8. Palette is out now.
  9. 2 lbs of bread, about 950 gram.
  10. Cut it into a half.
  11. Since the Bread is very wide, we will start to cut from the side of the loaf.
  12. Great after toast, enjoy with tea.
