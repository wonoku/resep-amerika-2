Ingredients of Sliced Pork Belly Wrapped Bell Peppers

  1. You need 400 g of Sliced Pork Belly (16 sheets).
  2. It's 1 pc of Red Bell Pepper.
  3. Prepare 1 pc of Yellow Bell Pepper.
  4. Prepare 1 pc of Green Bell Pepper.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of Salad Oil (cooking oil).
  6. You need 2 tbsp of Sake (Cooking Rice Wine).
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of Mirin.
  8. It's 2 tbsp of Soy Sauce.
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp of Sugar.
  10. It's As needed of Lead Lettuce.

Sliced Pork Belly Wrapped Bell Peppers instructions

  1. Peel the Red, Yellow, Green Bell Pepper skins. Cut each Bell Peppers to 16 equal parts in the vertical direction..
  2. Put three kinds of bell peppers on the pork and roll them up in the pork..
  3. Heat the Salad oil and start with the wrapped ends down. Cook while turning it over. When all of the meat become browned, wipe off excess oil with a paper towel..
  4. Add 2 tbsp Sake, 2tbsp Mirin, 2 tbsp Soy Sauce and 3 tbsp Sugar. Then place slid over the pan and steam cook the contents for few minutes over low heat..
  5. Cut the rolls in half diagonally and serve on a leaf lettuce..
