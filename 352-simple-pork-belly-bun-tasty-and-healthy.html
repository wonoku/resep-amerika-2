Simple Pork Belly Bun instructions

  2. Put the garlic and five spice in, add some water, and season..
  3. Bring to boil until the pork belly is tender and shimmer..
  4. Let it cool for about an hour..
  5. Steam the bun and reheat the pork belly right before eating..
