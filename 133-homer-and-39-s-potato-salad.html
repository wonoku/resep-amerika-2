Ingredients of Homer's Potato salad

  1. You need 8-10 of Golden Potatoes.
  2. You need 8-10 of Hard Boiled Eggs.
  3. Prepare 4 Tbsp of Mayonnaise.
  4. You need 4 Tbsp of Ranch Dressing.
  5. Prepare 3 Tbsp of Italian Dressing.
  6. You need 1 Tbsp of Brown sugar (Or regular).
  7. You need 1 Tsp of Black Pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 Tsp of Seasoning Salt or Salt.
  9. You need 3 Tbsp of Pickle Relish.
  10. You need 1/4 cup of Chopped Green onions.

Homer's Potato salad instructions

  1. Boil potatoes (until fork poke tender) Hard boil eggs.
  2. Chop eggs then mash with potatoes and dry ingredients (use potato masher or fork).
  3. Add wet ingredients and onions and continue to mash until desired consistency. Then chill..
  4. Enjoy!.
