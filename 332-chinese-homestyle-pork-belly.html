Ingredients of Chinese Homestyle Pork Belly

  1. It's 3/4 pound of pork belly cut into thin slices.

  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of onion powder.
  6. Prepare 1/8 tsp of white pepper powder.
  7. You need 2 stalks of green onion cut into 1 inch sections.

Chinese Homestyle Pork Belly instructions

  1. Marinate the pork belly slices with all the ingredients, except green onion, for at least one hour..
  2. Wrap the pork in a lightly greased aluminum foil. Air fry at 400F (200C) for 8 minutes..
  3. Unwrap the foil, stir the pork slices and air fry at 380F (190C) for about 4 minutes until the meat is cooked through..
  4. Add in the green onion and air fry again at 380F (190C) for 1-2 minute..
