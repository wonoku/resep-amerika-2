Ingredients of Tomatillo will rock you SALSA!

  1. It's 4 small of Tomatillos.
  2. It's 1 clove of Garlic.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of Cilantro.
  4. Prepare 1/2 large of Jalapeno.
  5. Prepare 1/4 slice of Avacado.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of White Onion.
  7. It's 1 tsp of Lime Juice.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of Chicken Broth (or water).
  9. Prepare 1 pinch of Salt and Pepper.

Tomatillo will rock you SALSA! instructions

  1. First char Tomatillos and jalapeno over gas stove flame, grill, or broiler, till you get some black color unevenly around veggies. slightly char garlic as well.
  2. set aside tomatillos, jalapeno and garlic..
  3. chop onion and cilantro and put into food processor or blender..
  4. cut tomatillos in half and add to cilantro and onion..
  5. add garlic to other ingrediants.
  6. cut jalapeno in half and deseed if desire less heat. add jalapeno to rest of ingredients.
  7. add lime juice, chicken broth, avacado and salt and pepper..
  8. blend ingredients untill completely mixed and have a soup consitancy. You can serve right then or put in fridge to let it set and flavors to meld..
  9. enjoy with steak, chicken or just chips and salsa!.
