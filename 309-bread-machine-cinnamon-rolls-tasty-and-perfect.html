Bread Machine Cinnamon Rolls instructions

  1. Add dough ingredients in order into bread machine.
  2. Turn on dough cycle and wait to finish.
  3. Grease 9x9x2 inch square pan.
  4. Mix 1/3 cup sugar and the cinnamon..
  5. Flatten dough with hands or rolling pin into 18x9 inch rectangle on lightly floured surface..
  6. Spread with 2 tablespoons butter.
  7. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture.
  8. Roll up rightly, beginning at 9 inch side.
  9. Pinch edge of dough into roll to seal. Cut roll into 1 inch slices.
  10. Place in pan. Cover; let rise in warm place 1 to 1/4 hours or until double.
  11. Heat oven to 375Â°. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown..
  12. Remove from pan; drizzle glaze over warm rolls.
