Ingredients of Pork Belly Breakfast

  1. Prepare of Grits---------------------.
  2. Prepare 1-1/2 cup of sharp cheddar cheese shredded.
  3. You need 4 cup of water.
  4. You need 1 cup of grits.
  5. Prepare 1/2 stick of butter.
  6. It's 1 teaspoon of kosher salt, for grits.
  7. Prepare of Eggs---------------.
  8. It's 2-1/2 tablespoons of butter.
  9. You need 6 of large eggs.
  10. It's To taste of kosher salt.
  11. Prepare of Pork Belly-------------.
  12. You need 1/4 pound of pork belly.
  13. You need 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt.
  14. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of ground sage.

Pork Belly Breakfast instructions

  1. Heat the water and add the salt. When boiling add the grits. Boil till done stirring often so as to not having lumps. Add the butter and cheese..
  2. Stir in and Cover off heat..
  3. Cut the pork belly fry in a pan with salt and sage..
  4. Turn as needed and season the second side..
  5. Fry crispy.
  6. Beat the eggs with salt and add the butter to the pan and fry till done. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
