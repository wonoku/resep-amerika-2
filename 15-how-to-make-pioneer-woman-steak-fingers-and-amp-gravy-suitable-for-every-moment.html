Ingredients of Pioneer Woman Steak Fingers & Gravy

  1. It's 1 of Steak.
  2. It's 1 cup of Flour.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of Seasoned Salt.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Black Pepper.
  5. You need 1/4 tsp of Cayenne.
  6. It's 2 of Eggs.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of Milk.
  8. It's 2 tbsp of Butter.
  9. It's of Canola Oil.

Pioneer Woman Steak Fingers & Gravy step by step

  1. Cut steak into strips..
  2. Put butter and canola oil in a pan on high..
  3. Put dry ingredients in one bowl..
  4. Put wet ingredients in a separate bowl. Dredge the steak strips in flour mix first then the egg then back in the flour..
  5. Add steak strips to the butter and oil pan. 2 minutes per side..
  6. Once they are done put them on a paper towel to soak up grease..
  7. Reserve 1/4 cup of grease mixture for gravy and dump the rest..
  8. Put it back into the pan with 2 tbs of access flour mixture or fresh flour then add milk as needed to desired consistency. I probably added at least 3/4 cup..
  9. This is how my gravy turned out..
  10. Serve and enjoy:).
