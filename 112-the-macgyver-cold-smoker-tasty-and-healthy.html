The MacGyver Cold Smoker step by step

  1. Soak wood chips in your favorite liquid (water, beer, wine, etc.) for 3 hours..
  2. Fill the can with 1/2 cup of wood chips at a time and place in the firebox of your offset smoker on it's side..
  3. Insert the tip of the soldering iron into the can, underneath the wood chips, to the back of the can..
  4. Plug the soldering iron in and turn it on..
  5. The time it takes to begin seeing wisps of smoke depends on the wattage of the soldering iron. I used the 40 watt setting on my iron and began seeing smoke in about 5 minutes..
  6. After 15 minutes, there will be smoking embers. Turn off or unplug the soldering iron..
  7. Keep the firebox vent open by a quarter and the smoke chamber damper by an eighth..
  8. I was very pleased how well this worked. 1/2 cup of wood chips generated copious amounts of smoke for about 1.5 hours.
