Fried liver with okra instructions

  1. Heat pan, put in your oil and heat a bit then add your onion slices and saute.
  2. Add some garlic/ginger paste and fry slightly.
  3. Once done add your liver and stir well, let it cook till the juices are dry then add in your tomatoes at this stage.
  4. Let it cook till almost but not completely dry. Add the salt at this stage and set aside..
  5. For the okra. Heat pan, add oil and saute your onion slices.
  6. Add the diced okra and some salt and stir well. Don't overcook please!.
