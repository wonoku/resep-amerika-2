Ingredients of American potato salad

  1. Prepare 2 of potatoes cooked in salted water.
  2. Prepare 1 of cooked beet.
  3. You need of Lemon juice (1 tablespoon).
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of green onion cubes.
  5. Prepare 2 of hard- boiled eggs cut in cubes.
  6. You need 1 pinch of chopped mint.
  7. It's of Salt.
  8. You need 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.
  9. It's 1/2 tablespoon of pickles.
  10. It's 1/2 tablespoon of chopped parsley.
  11. You need 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder.
  12. You need 1/2 teaspoon of mustard.
  13. Prepare 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise.
  14. You need tablespoon of celery in cubes.

American potato salad instructions

  1. Mix the hot potatoes with the lemon juice and salt and mint, the onion powder, black pepper and do the same for the cooked beet for good absorption of the spices and lemon juice, then put them in the refrigerator,.
  2. Once the mixture has cooled, add the chopped parsley and pickles, the eggs, the celery cut into cubes and a tablespoon of mayonnaise and 1/2 teaspoon of mustard.
  3. Put the mixture in an oval plate and decorate with the stripes of cubes of beetroot and chopped parsley and the tomato flower..
