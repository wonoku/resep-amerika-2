Ingredients of Jalapeno popper Hasselbeck potatoes

  1. You need 2 of medium Idaho potatoes.
  2. You need 2 Tablespoons of olive oil.
  3. You need 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt.
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of cracked black pepper.
  5. You need 2 slices of bacon.
  6. You need 1 Tablespoon of pickled jalapeno.
  7. It's 2 ounces of cream cheese.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese.

Jalapeno popper Hasselbeck potatoes step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cut thin lines down the potatoes. Use two wooden spoons laid next to the potato as a guide if needed..
  2. Drizzle potatoes with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake until the potatoes are fanned out, around 60 minutes..
  3. Chop bacon and cook until crispy. Drain on paper towel and set aside. Stir cream cheese and jalapenos together in bowl. Set aside..
  4. Fill each slot with bacon. Cover each potato with cream cheese and jalapeno mixture then top with cheddar cheese..
  5. Bake potatoes for 10 minutes then broil until cheddar cheese is browned and bubbly, around 2 minutes..
