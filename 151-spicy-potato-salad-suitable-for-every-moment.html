Spicy potato salad step by step

  1. Boil potatoes until fork tender peel and dice.
  2. Dice 4 eggs, slice remaining eggs for garnish.
  3. Place in large bowl, add remaining ingredients except garnish.
  4. Mix well, taste and adjust seasonings.
  5. Place slices of egg, and sprinkle smoked paprika on top.
  6. Chill for 1hour or longer.
  7. The spices blend more the longer you chill it.
  8. Enjoy.
