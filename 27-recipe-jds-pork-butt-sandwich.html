The garlic cooks quickly in 20 seconds (maybe less as it depends on how hot the pan is). If the garlic is cooked too long, it will taste not quite agreeable and shed its aroma. If you would like to add garlic, then be sure you add it last, so it doesn't burn.

Ingredients of JDs Pork Butt Sandwich

  1. You need 3 lb of Pork Butt Roast, bone removed.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of Olive Oil.
  3. You need 3 tbsp of Garlic, Minced.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of Italian Seasonings.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of Sea Salt.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Cracked Black Pepper.
  7. You need 1 of Orange Zest from med. Orange.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp of Fennel Seeds.
  9. It's 2 tsp of Chili Flakes.
  10. Prepare 1 of Salt.
  11. You need 1 of Pepper.
  12. Prepare 1 of Porcinis.
  13. You need 1 cup of Beer.
  14. Prepare 1 of Mozzerella Cheese.
  15. You need 1 of Provelone Cheese.
  16. It's 1 of Cherry Tomatoes, sliced in half.
  17. It's 6 of Hoagie Buns, toasted.

JDs Pork Butt Sandwich step by step

  1. Sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper on fatty side of pork butt. Flip over..
  2. Rub inside, meaty side, with garlic, italian seasoning, sea salt, cracked pepper, fennel seed, chili flake, and orange zest. Sprinkle with approx. 2 tbls. olive oil..
  3. Roll up fatty side out and secure. Cover and refrigerate overnite..
  4. Bake at 350° for about 5 hours covered, uncover and cook 1/2 hour longer..
  5. Cool slightly and cut into thin slices..
  6. Heat in pan with beer and sliced porcinnis, heat through..
  7. Top with cheeses and allow to melt. Transfer to toasted hoagies and top with sliced cherry tomatoes..
  8. *suggestion: spread basil pesto aioli to hoagie, add fresh fried sweet potato fries on the side, and pop a top. * add onions, peppers, sauteed mushrooms..
