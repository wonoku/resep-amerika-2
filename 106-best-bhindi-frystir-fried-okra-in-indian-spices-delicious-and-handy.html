Ingredients of bhindi fry(stir fried okra in indian spices)

  1. You need 500 grams of okra(lady's finger) sliced (top & bottom removed).
  2. You need 1 tsp of cumin seeds.
  3. Prepare 2 large of onions(chopped finely).
  4. It's 4 clove of garlic(ground coarsely).
  5. Prepare 1/2 of inch ginger(ground coarsely).
  6. It's 1 tsp of black mustard seeds.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of cumin powder.
  8. You need 1 tsp of coriander powder.
  9. It's 1 tsp of turmeric powder.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of vegetable oil.
  11. You need 1 dash of garam masala.
  12. You need 1 of salt to taste.
  13. It's 3 of green chilies(chopped finely).

bhindi fry(stir fried okra in indian spices) step by step

  1. heat oil in a saucepan or wok til it just starts to smoke..
  2. add cumin seeds & mustard seeds.when they start to crackle quickly add garlic ginger & chilies.fry til garlic light golden.
  3. add onions & fry til golden..
  4. add all spices (except garam masala) & salt.add a sprinkle a lil water so that the spices dont burn.
  5. then add all bhindi(okra) and fry til it loses its stickines & brite green color stirring lightly occasionaly.
  6. caution:dont cover d pan.
  7. lastly add garam masala mix once & serve.
  8. tip:ginger & garlic ground in a mortar usin a pestle wil giv best results & take care while chopping chili & cracklin seeds.
  9. cn b served with chapaties or rice or nan or tanduri rotis.
