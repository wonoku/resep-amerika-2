Ingredients of Instapot Chicken Rice & Beans

  1. You need 2 tbsp of Olive oil.
  2. It's of 1/4 cup chopped onion.
  3. You need 2 clove of garlic.
  4. Prepare 2 of Chicken Breasts.
  5. It's 1 Cup of Rice.
  6. You need of 1/2 Cup water.
  7. Prepare 1/2 Cup of chicken broth.
  8. Prepare of Tspn salt.
  9. You need of Half tspn pepper.
  10. It's 1 Can of Black Beans.
  11. You need 1 Cups of Broccoli.

Instapot Chicken Rice & Beans step by step

  1. Chop broccoli florets.
  2. Place florets and cup of water in instapot. Set on high pressure with a time of zero..
  3. Quick release and place broccoli in a separate bowl, cover and set aside.
  4. Slice chicken into cubes.
  5. Chop onion and garlic clove.
  6. Turn instapot to saute and add olive oil, onion, garlic and chicken.
  7. Once chicken loses it's raw color add water, broth, rice, pepper and salt; stir in lightly..
  8. Cook on manual high pressure for 6 min and quick release.
  9. Leave instapot on keep warm and add broccoli and drained black beans. Stir and let sit for 3 minutes..
