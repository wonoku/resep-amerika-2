Ingredients of Tomatillo Braised Turkey Tacos

  1. You need 5 lb of bone-in turkey breast.
  2. It's 2 C of tomatillo salsa.
  3. You need 2 of yellow onions; large julienne.
  4. Prepare 2 of carrots; peeled & cut thin on bias.
  5. You need 1 of jalapeño; halved & thick julienne.
  6. You need 4 cloves of garlic; slivered.
  7. It's 2 t of Mexican oregano.
  8. It's 1/4 C of apple cider vinegar.
  9. Prepare 2 t of cumin.
  10. You need 1 t of ground coriander seed.
  11. Prepare 1 t of onion powder.
  12. Prepare 1 t of garlic powder.
  13. You need 1 t of ground celery seed.
  14. It's 1 of drizzle extra virgin olive oil.
  15. You need 1 t of paprika.
  16. You need as needed of kosher salt & black pepper.

Tomatillo Braised Turkey Tacos step by step

  2. De-bone turkey breast. Remove skin..
  3. In a small, deep casserole dish lay onions across the bottom. Place carrots, jalapeños, apple cider vinegar, garlic, and salsa atop. Mix together..
  4. Lay turkey breasts on top. Drizzle olive oil over the turkey. Season..
