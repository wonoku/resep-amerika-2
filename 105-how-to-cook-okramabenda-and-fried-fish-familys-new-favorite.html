Ingredients of Okra(mabenda) and fried fish

  1. Prepare 10 pieces of fresh okra(mabenda).
  2. Prepare 1 piece of grated carrot.
  3. It's 1 piece of fish.
  4. It's of Cupsicum.
  5. You need 2 of tomatoes.
  6. You need 2 of large onions.
  7. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  8. You need of Maize floor for ugali.

Okra(mabenda) and fried fish step by step

  1. Wash the Okra very well.fresh ones are soft not hard.
  2. Cut them into pieces.
  3. In a pan add 2 teaspoon of cooking oil.fry the onions to golden brown.add the Okra and salt to taste.stir and remove after 5 minutes.
  4. Remember it is a vegetable so it's not supposed to over cook.The crunchier the better.
  5. In a pan add cooking oil and once it's hot add the washed fish and deep fry.
  6. In a pan fry the onions,add the cupsicum then tomatoes and carrots.cover to let the tomatoes be soft.add 1/2 a cup of water and salt to taste.
  7. Once it has heated add the fish.let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Serve with ugali while hot.
