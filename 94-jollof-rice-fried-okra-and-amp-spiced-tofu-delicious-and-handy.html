Ingredients of Jollof Rice, Fried Okra & Spiced Tofu

  1. You need 4 cup of rice.
  2. You need 6 of fresh tomatoes.
  3. You need 1 of bell pepper.
  4. Prepare 2 cup of vegetable stock.
  5. You need 1 of large onion.
  6. You need 2 of red chillis.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of chipotle paste.
  8. It's 2 cm of ginger.
  9. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic.
  10. It's 200 g of okra.
  11. Prepare 300 g of firm tofu.
  12. It's 1 tbsp of curry powder.
  13. You need 1 tsp of nutmeg.
  14. You need 2 tsp of smoked paprika.
  15. You need 5 sprigs of thyme.
  16. You need 1 tbsp of cumin.
  17. It's 1 tbsp of cornstarch.
  18. Prepare to taste of Salt.

Jollof Rice, Fried Okra & Spiced Tofu step by step

  1. Chop the onion, pepper, tomatoes, chilli, ginger & garlic.
  2. Slice okra lengthwise, coat in oil, cornstarch, cumin & paprika.
  3. Coat the tofu in oil and cumin.
  4. Blend ingredients, and add the chipotle paste.
  5. Heat some oil in a pot, and gently fry the paste..
  6. Add curry powder, nutmeg, and tomato puree. Cook for a couple more minutes.
  7. Add the rice and cook for a couple of minutes.
  8. Add the stock, thyme and bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer. Cook for about 15/20 minutes.
  9. While the rice is cooking, heat some oil in a skillet and fry the okra.
  10. Do the same for the tofu.
  11. Once it's all nicely cooked, serve!.
