Ingredients of Vegan hash browns

  1. Prepare 4 cups of frozen shredded potatoes.
  2. You need 1/2 of medium onion, chopped.
  3. You need 1 cup of diced tomatoes.
  4. Prepare 1 tbs of minced garlic.
  5. It's 2 handfuls of fresh spinach.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of chopped mushrooms.
  7. Prepare 2 tbs of olive oil.
  8. You need to taste of Salt, pepper.
  9. Prepare of Avocado and salsa for garnish.
