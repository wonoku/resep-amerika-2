Ingredients of Cauliflower soup

  1. Prepare 1 head of cauliflower.
  2. Prepare 4 of each chicken bouillon cubes.
  3. You need 3 tbsp of cream of wheat.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of butter or margarine.
  5. You need 1 packages of kielbasa or beef smoked sausage sliced (optional).
  6. Prepare 7 cup of water.
  7. It's 1 of salt and pepper to taste (optional).

Cauliflower soup instructions

  1. Cut and wash cauliflower, add to a pot and add water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender..
  2. Add chicken bouillon. Add more for flavor if you like..
  3. If using meat, add now. Return to a boil. Cook 5 minutes..
  4. Add cream of wheat. This can be adjusted, more for thicker soup..
  5. Add butter, Boil for 3 minutes.
