Rice brunch instructions

  1. Add cooked rice, oil and cabbage on low-medium heat, cover..
  2. Stir occasionally until rice browns.
  3. In separate pan fry eggs (scramble can be added to rice).
  4. Add cheese.
  5. Let stand till cheese is melted.
  6. Enjoy.
