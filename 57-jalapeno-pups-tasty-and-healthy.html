Ingredients of Jalapeño Pups

  1. It's 2 cup of self-rising cornbread mix.
  2. Prepare 1 of large jalapeño pepper.
  3. You need 1 of medium onion.
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  5. You need 1 teaspoon of salt.
  6. It's 1 cup of water.
  7. Prepare 2 of large eggs.
  8. It's As needed of oil to deep fry in.

Jalapeño Pups step by step

  1. Heat oil in a deep thick pot. This will help stabilize the temperature of the oil..
  2. Wash and remove the seeds and ribbing of the jalapeño peppers..
  3. Chop the onion then add to a mixing bowl..
  4. Add the spices, cornbread mix, and eggs.
  5. Add the water and mix well. You want it thick..
  6. Take a tablespoon scoop at a time. Drop carefully into the hot oil. Deep fry turning as necessary. Cook in batches and don't overcrowd the pot..
  7. When each batch is done add to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil..
  8. Repeat till done..
  9. Serve I hope you enjoy. It can be served with many different dishes. As a snack also, with your favorite jelly or jam..
