Peppery chicken with a plum glaze instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Cut each breast in half lengthwise, making 12 breast portions..
  2. Add breast to oil add half salt and pepper to top side of chicken and cover..
  3. Turn the chicken after 7 minutes add rest of pepper and salt,. Cover and cook..
  4. The liquid will reduce and chicken will brown turn and brown the top side..
  5. Remove from heat covered..
  6. Peel the plums.
  7. Remove the pits.
  8. Cube the plums.
  9. Heat butter.
  10. Add plums.
  11. After simmering 3 minutes add jaggery..
  12. Simmer till thickens.
  13. Mash the plums a bit continue to let thicken..
  14. Pour on top of the chicken bring to a simmer stirring occasionally till chicken is evenly coated. Let rest 7 minutes and serve I hope you enjoy!.
